About Me

The engineer behind the work

I'm a frontend engineer with over 7 years of experience specializing in Angular and modern web development. Over the years, I've worked on building scalable, maintainable, and high-performance applications, particularly those involving complex user interfaces and data-heavy interactions.

My core strength lies in translating real-world problems into efficient frontend architectures. I enjoy working on challenging UI systems—such as dynamic forms, grid-based interfaces, and real-time data handling—where performance and user experience are critical.

Currently, I'm expanding my skill set by diving deeper into backend development using NestJS and exploring how AI can be integrated into modern web applications. My goal is to evolve into a well-rounded engineer who can design and build end-to-end systems while staying aligned with emerging technologies.
